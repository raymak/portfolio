Ephemeral Launcher

Ephemeral Launcher improves visual search for mobile apps by using a highlighting technique called ephemeral adaptation .

Characteristics
  • improves search speed performance by 8-10%
  • does not compromise aesthetics
  • is qualitatively preferred by users over conventional screens
  • works with both low-accuracy and high-accuracy prediction algorithms
  • works with both small and large number of pages of icons

Tools we implemented Ephemeral Launcher as an Android app in two demo and experiment versions.
